How it works

The wait times nobody else has.

Resort sites tell you a lift is "open." They never tell you it's a 20-minute wait. PeakWait fills that gap with the one source that actually knows — the skiers standing in it.

1

One tap says Moving, Short, Busy, or Long. It takes a second — your day auto-tracks the rest.

2

PeakWait reads the crowd

Reports blend with official status and live weather into a confidence-rated wait band — never a fake exact number.

3

You get the best next lift

The shortest wait worth skiing to from where you stand — so you spend the day riding, not waiting.
