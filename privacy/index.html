Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026 · Effective: July 22, 2026

What PeakWait is

PeakWait helps skiers see lift status and estimated line lengths (shown as ranges, or "bands") and find a good next lift, across resorts. This policy explains what we collect and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Precise location — only during an active ski session. When a ski session is active, we use your device location to show nearby lifts, let you report lines near a lift, and (anonymously) improve estimates. Outside a session we do not collect precise location.
  • Queue reports you submit — a band like "Short" or "Busy," with the approximate location and time needed to validate the report.
  • An anonymous device identifier — kept separate from your account, used to rate-limit and detect abusive or coordinated reports. You can reset it in Settings.
  • Account info, if you sign in — Sign in with Apple provides an identifier and, if you choose, a display name. We keep account identity separate from analytics identifiers.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not use your location for advertising.
  • We do not sell precise individual location histories.
  • We do not require your real name.

How long we keep it

  • Raw precise coordinates are converted into lift/queue events quickly and purged on a retention schedule — precise points are removed from stored reports after a short window.
  • Aggregated, de-identified queue history may be retained to improve predictions.

Your choices and rights

  • Delete my history and Export my history are available in the app (Settings → Your data).
  • Reset anonymous device ID unlinks future reports from past ones.
  • You can turn off location any time in iOS Settings; PeakWait still shows official status.
  • California residents have additional rights (access, deletion, and limits on certain sharing). To exercise them, contact contact@peakwait.net.

Children

PeakWait is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Third-party data

PeakWait shows lift and resort status sourced from licensed and public feeds through our own backend. No third-party advertising or analytics SDKs collect your location.

Safety

PeakWait never overrides posted signs, patrol instructions, or closures. Recommendations are advisory and are suppressed when data is inadequate. PeakWait does not provide off-piste or backcountry routing.
